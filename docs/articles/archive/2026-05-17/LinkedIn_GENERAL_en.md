# LinkedIn Post — 2026-05-17 General Audience (English)

🤖 **From "just using AI" to "giving AI a secretary" — FullSense, the local-first AI framework**

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini are convenient — but do these worries sound familiar?

❓ Is it safe to send corporate secrets to cloud AI?
❓ What about family conversations?
❓ Where does medical record data actually go?
❓ What if the network goes down?
❓ Who's accountable when AI makes a mistake?

I'm building **FullSense ™** (a trio of OSS projects: llmesh + llive + llove) to tackle these head-on.

💡 **Concept**: If AI is "a brilliant but forgetful, math-weak, low-accountability new hire," then llive is "**not a new AI core, but a secretary that knows how to use AI properly**."

✨ **What you can do with it**:
- ✅ Run entirely on your local PC — zero cloud uploads
- ✅ Append-only ledger records every decision (auditable later)
- ✅ Dangerous actions gated by a human approval flow
- ✅ Calculations delegated to a built-in calculator (no LLM math errors)
- ✅ Unit-dimension checks (auto-rejects "5 m/s + 3 s" type mistakes)
- ✅ Keeps working when the internet is down

🎯 **Use cases**:
- Clinical record analysis (stays inside the hospital)
- Legal document organization (confidentiality preserved)
- Financial decision logging (audit-ready)
- Family conversation summaries (privacy intact)
- Manufacturing measurement decisions (direct IoT integration)

⚖️ **Accountability built in**: AI proposes, humans approve / reject / edit through the TUI (llove). Never fully autonomous — humans stay in the loop.
