We face a contradiction in the lltrade design: we want to maximise strategy responsiveness (low latency) while also enforcing comprehensive risk checks on every signal (high reliability). One approach is segmentation — split each signal into a fast pre-check and a slower comprehensive check. Another is asymmetry — apply heavier checks only on high-impact signals, light checks otherwise. Propose a resolution that uses both segmentation and asymmetry while keeping the audit ledger complete. Constraints: max drawdown 10%, sharpe target 1.5, paper-trading-only.
